Welcome to Srinivasa High School

A school is a temple of Learning. It's a place that modifies the lives of children & develops their character. We give equal importance to both academics & building good character. Under our guidance, our staff works as a good team and works hard to ensure the abilities of the children are nurtured & carefully developed. Every child has unique qualities & capabilities.
